Privacy Policy

How we handle your information.

This policy explains the kinds of personal information BlueCarter Enterprise may receive through this website, why it is collected, and how it is used in the ordinary course of responding to enquiries and providing services.

Overview

BlueCarter Enterprise is a digital services, graphic design, branding, and premium printing business. When you contact us through this website, email us directly, or otherwise share your information with us, we may collect and use that information to respond to your request, prepare quotes, manage projects, and improve how we communicate with prospective and existing clients.

Information We May Collect

Depending on how you interact with the site, we may receive information such as:

  • Your name and contact details, including email address or phone number.
  • Business information relevant to your enquiry, such as company name or service interest.
  • Project details you choose to share when requesting a quote or describing work.
  • Basic technical information generated by normal website use, such as browser or device data.

How We Use Information

Information shared with us may be used to:

  • Respond to questions and quote requests.
  • Discuss project requirements and deliver services.
  • Maintain business records connected to current or prospective work.
  • Improve the clarity, performance, and usefulness of this website.

Information Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only where reasonably necessary to operate the business, such as with service providers, collaborators, hosting providers, or professional advisers supporting delivery, administration, or compliance. We may also disclose information where required by law or to protect legitimate business interests.

Data Retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary to handle enquiries, manage projects, maintain records, and meet legal or operational obligations. The exact retention period will depend on the nature of the relationship and the information involved.

Your Choices

If you would like to update, correct, or request deletion of information you have shared with us, contact us using the details on the main website. We will review reasonable requests in light of any legal, contractual, or operational requirements that may apply.

Third-Party Services

This website may rely on third-party tools such as font providers, email clients, analytics, or hosting infrastructure. Those services may process limited technical information as part of normal website delivery. Their handling of data will be governed by their own policies and service terms.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as the business, website, or operational requirements evolve. The version published on this page should be treated as the current one.
